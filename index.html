Over time this declared the supremacy of the federation as its economic policies ushered forth greater technologies of war as the confederation's anti-capitalistic policies left it behind.

Giga: Means giant and refers to mechanical units called mobile suits. These suits are mainly used for mining and heavy lifting and employ a miniature nuclear engine to power it. Many companies employ such devices, it is only recently have they shown to have military applications (7 BI. Dilmun ).

Zionic Expulsion: Dilmun calls for desertion of the "Zionic Lands" as it has caused disputes between nations. Dilmun sets a date for the cities inside the lands “illumination”. Lycanthropy guards essential installations within the lands to protect them from nuclear detonation. They fail when Thalatto Marduk and Dilmun destroy these installations and abduct Doctor Ananko (5 BI).

Operation Exodus: Mass emigration plan using the Incident as a catalyst to create cheap labor (8 AI).

Incident: Using Xisuthrus, Dilmun ignites every ICBM across the world starting the last world war. This incident is claimed by Dilmun "enlightening the world". The loss of life is staggering and the resulting blame game initiates a war, ending the confederation. At first intended to create peace by disabling every ICBM but the plan changed when Draco and then Thalatto combined with Xisuthrus.

Prime Minister Draco: Late Prime Minister and General of the federation. Ultimately the reason for Marduk's disgust with politics and creation of Dilmun. His assassination was the first act of Dilmun, though his supporters act in his memory. Syn Draco takes his brain and connects him to Xisuthrus which gives him everlasting life which was the last wish of the Prime Minister.

Doctor Marduk: Responsible for Atomic Pressurization and Conversion Tech and also contributed to the introduction of Gigas. He later defected to the Confederation and lost his wife and child in the process (30 BI). He resides in Neo Babylon, a shell of his former self and nearly complete with Xisuthrus.

Thalassa Marduk: Late wife of Doctor Marduk and mother of Syn. She was said to be an inspiration to Marduk.

Thalatto Marduk: Genetically modified male clone of Thalassa Marduk with a Y-chromosome from the doctor. He is plagued with headaches but is talented on and off the battlefield. He respects his father but considers all of humanity beneath him. When confronted with the sentiment that he may not be perfect draws his rage.

Syn "Draco": Actual child Doctor Marduk and Thalassa. Though her mother died before she was born the child was saved and trained as an agent of espionage for the federation. Works alongside Thalatto and she plays into his hands.
